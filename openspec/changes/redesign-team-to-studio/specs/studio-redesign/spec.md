# Spec: Studio Section Redesign

## Requirements
- **Copy Update**:
    - Section Label: "The Studio" / "Nuestro Estudio".
    - Title: "Why Symmetrical Code?" / "¿Por qué Symmetrical Code?".
    - Subtitle: "We combine engineering expertise with a focus on business results to deliver high-quality custom software."
- **Content Blocks**:
    - **Engineering Standards**: Focus on clean code, testing, and scalability.
    - **UI/UX Architecture**: Focus on conversion-oriented design and performance.
    - **Reliable Delivery**: Focus on our process, task division, and transparency.
- **Visuals**:
    - Remove all profile images (`lalo.png`, `pato.png`, `mario.png`).
    - Use abstract icons or stylized graphics that represent "Synergy" and "Scale".
    - Remove the modal interaction entirely.

## Scenarios
- **Scenario 1: Trust Building**
    - A potential client should immediately understand HOW we work (task division) and WHY they can trust us (engineering standards).
- **Scenario 2: Responsive Quality**
    - The new blocks must look great on mobile, potentially stacking the pillars vertically.
- **Scenario 3: Language Alignment**
    - All business-focused copy must be properly translated in both `es.json` and `en.json`.
